- **Theo 3.4 - Parseval equality:** $f:\mathbb{R}\rightarrow\mathbb{C}$ be a $2\pi$ piecewise continuous function. Then,
$$\sum_{n=-\infty}^\infty |c_n(f)|^2=\frac{1}{2\pi}\int_0^{2\pi}|f(x)|^2dx$$
